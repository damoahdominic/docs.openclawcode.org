## How credits work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up" icon="user-plus">
    Every new account starts with **$5.00 in free credits** — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use your agent" icon="robot">
    Each interaction with your AI agent uses a small amount of credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up when needed" icon="cart-shopping">
    When your balance runs low, buy more from your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

|---------|-------|
| $5 | $5 |

| $20 | $20 |
| $50 | $50 |

<Info>Credits never expire. Use them at your own pace.</Info>

## How far do credits go?

| What you're doing | Cost per interaction | How many with $5 |
|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Quick questions | ~$0.001 | ~5,000 |
| Moderate tasks | ~$0.003 | ~1,600 |
| Longer conversations | ~$0.01 | ~500 |
| Complex tasks | ~$0.05 | ~100 |

Most people find that **$5 lasts a long time** for everyday use.

## Buying credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard" icon="chart-line">
    Visit [your dashboard](https://occ.mba.sh/dashboard) and click **Credits** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick an amount" icon="hand-pointer">
    Choose $5, $10, $20, or $50.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay securely" icon="lock">
    Complete checkout via Stripe — accepts cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits added instantly" icon="circle-check">
    Your balance updates immediately. Start using your agent right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens when credits run out?

When your balance reaches $0:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Agent pauses" icon="pause">
    Your AI agent can't process new requests until you top up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Nothing is lost" icon="shield-halved">
    Just add credits and pick up where you left off. Everything else keeps working.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
