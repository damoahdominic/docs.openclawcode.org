## How Credits Work

OCCode uses a simple **prepaid credit system**. You load credits into your account, and they're used as you interact with your AI agent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Every new account starts with **$5.00 in free credits** — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use your agent">
    Each interaction with your AI agent uses a small amount of credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up when needed">
    When your balance runs low, buy more credits from the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Far Do Credits Go?

Your credits are used based on how much your AI agent processes. Here's a rough guide:

| What you're doing | Approximate cost | How many with $5 |
|-------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Quick questions | ~$0.001 each | ~5,000 |
| Moderate tasks | ~$0.003 each | ~1,600 |
| Longer conversations | ~$0.01 each | ~500 |
| Complex tasks | ~$0.05 each | ~100 |

Most people find that **$5 lasts a long time** for everyday use.

## Buying Credits

### From the Dashboard

1. Go to [occ.mba.sh/dashboard](https://occ.mba.sh/dashboard)
2. Click **Credits** in the sidebar
3. Pick a credit amount ($5 / $10 / $20 / $50)
4. Complete payment via **Stripe** — fast, secure checkout
5. Credits are added to your account instantly

### Accepted Payment Methods

Stripe supports:
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Bank transfers (select regions)

## Checking Your Balance

### In the Dashboard

The [dashboard](https://occ.mba.sh/dashboard) shows your current balance front and center on the **Overview** page, along with your total usage and request count.

### In OCCode

Your balance is shown within the app and updates automatically.

## What Happens When Credits Run Out?

When your balance reaches **$0.00**:

- Your AI agent pauses — it can't process new requests
- You'll see a prompt to add credits
- **Nothing is lost** — just top up and pick up where you left off
- OCCode itself continues to work normally

## Viewing Your History

The dashboard keeps a complete record:

- **Usage** page — every interaction, with cost and timestamp
- **Credits** page — every purchase, with amount and date
