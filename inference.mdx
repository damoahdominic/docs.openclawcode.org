## The Big Picture

When you use OCCode, the app communicates with your AI agent through a cloud service that handles authentication and billing automatically. You don't need to configure anything — it just works.

```
Your Computer                    Cloud                        AI
┌──────────┐              ┌──────────────┐             ┌──────────┐
│  OCCode  │─── request ──▶  occ.mba.sh  │── forward ──▶  Agent   │
│   App    │◀── response ─│  (gateway)   │◀── reply ───│ (OpenClaw)│
└──────────┘              └──────────────┘             └──────────┘
                           ✓ Checks your login
                           ✓ Checks your credits
                           ✓ Deducts usage cost
```

## What Happens When You Use Your Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="You make a request">
    You interact with your AI agent through OCCode — ask it a question, give it a task, or check on something.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OCCode sends it to the cloud">
    The request goes to `occ.mba.sh`, which verifies your account and checks that you have enough credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your agent responds">
    The request is forwarded to your OpenClaw AI agent, which processes it and sends back a response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are deducted">
    A small amount is deducted from your credit balance based on how much the agent processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage Costs

Each interaction with your agent uses a small amount of credits based on how much text is processed:

| Activity | Approximate Cost |
|----------|-----------------|
| Quick question | ~$0.001 |
| Moderate task | ~$0.003 |
| Longer conversation | ~$0.01 |
| Complex task | ~$0.05 |

With **$5 in free credits**, you can have thousands of interactions with your agent.

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

| What you see | What it means | What to do |
|-------------|---------------|------------|
| "Credits depleted" | Your balance is $0 | [Top up credits](https://occ.mba.sh/dashboard) |
| "Session expired" | You need to sign in again | Sign in through OCCode |
| "Service unavailable" | Temporary cloud issue | Wait a moment and try again |
