## The big picture

When you interact with your AI agent through OCCode, everything is handled automatically behind the scenes. You don't need to configure anything.

```
  You  ──▶  OCCode App  ──▶  Cloud Service  ──▶  Your AI Agent
                              ✓ Verifies you
                              ✓ Checks credits
                              ✓ Deducts usage
```

---

## Step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="You interact with your agent" icon="message">
    Ask a question, give a task, or check on something through OCCode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OCCode sends it securely" icon="paper-plane">
    Your request goes to the cloud service, which verifies your account and checks your credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your agent responds" icon="robot">
    The request reaches your OpenClaw AI agent, which processes it and sends back a response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are deducted" icon="coins">
    A small amount is deducted from your balance based on how much the agent processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How much does it cost?

Each interaction uses a tiny amount of credits:

| What you're doing | Approximate cost |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| Quick question | ~$0.001 |
| Moderate task | ~$0.003 |
| Longer conversation | ~$0.01 |
| Complex task | ~$0.05 |

With **$5 in free credits**, you can have **thousands of interactions** with your agent.
