## 2. Sign In

When you open OCCode for the first time, you'll be prompted to sign in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Sign In'">
    OCCode opens your browser to sign in securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your method">
    Sign in with **Google** (one click) or create an account with **email + password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    After signing in, you're automatically redirected back to OCCode — ready to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Every new account gets **$5.00 in free credits** — enough to get your agent up and running right away.
</Info>

## 3. Set Up Your Agent

Once signed in, OCCode walks you through getting your OpenClaw agent set up:

- **Setup wizard** — follow the guided steps to configure and connect your agent
- **Status panel** — see at a glance whether your agent is running and healthy
- **Troubleshooting** — if something goes wrong, OCCode helps you fix it

No terminal commands. No config files. Just follow the prompts.

## 4. Check Your Dashboard

Visit [occ.mba.sh/dashboard](https://occ.mba.sh/dashboard) anytime to:

- View your **credit balance**
- See your **usage history**
- **Buy more credits** when you need them
